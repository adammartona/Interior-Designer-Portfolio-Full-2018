Architecture & Interior Design


Placing furnitures is like a chess game, where the objects effect each other.
My goal was to find these interactions balanced version. For a comfortable room,
the objects placed based on their using frequency. The room designed around the
furnitures, so it has just the right size. Various materials and furnitures has
big impact on each other visually. These selected elements fine tuned composition
give the coziness and aesthetics of the room. I tried different variations to find a valuable one.
