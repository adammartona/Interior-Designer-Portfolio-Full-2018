about

Simulating our world's visual phenomenons open up huge oppurtunities. The generated light effects reflection, diversity, complexity, can make the render look like real a photo. This is especially true for interiors. The renders give impression about the coziness and effectiveness of the room. For me, making visualizations mean more than photorealistic render of the plan. It’s a form of art, like photography, with more option.

Design: weekend house, automotive body, sofa set, office furniture, kinder toy, coffee mug, sport tool, sunbed
Modelling: cars, airplane, furnitures, plants, cloth objects, exhibition booth, etc.
Animation: abstract, character
